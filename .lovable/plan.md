

# Digital Wedding Invitation — Pedro & Julia 💍

## Visual Identity
- **Color palette**: Cream (#FDFBF7) background, Sage Green (#8A9A5B) accents, soft Gold (#D4AF37) for borders and icons
- **Typography**: Playfair Display for headings, Great Vibes script for the couple's names, clean sans-serif for body text
- **Graphics**: Thin line-art botanical/floral corner illustrations throughout sections

---

## Sections (in scroll order)

### 1. Interactive Envelope Opening
A full-screen Sage Green envelope with a gold wax seal monogrammed "P&J." On click, the envelope fades out and scales up to reveal the wedding invitation content beneath.

### 2. Hero / Main Header
Full-screen section with a faded Mediterranean venue background image. Displays "We Are Getting Married," the couple's names "Pedro & Julia" in large script, and the date "May 9, 2026."

### 3. Countdown Timer
Minimalist countdown to May 9, 2026 showing Days, Hours, Minutes, and Seconds in elegant styled boxes.

### 4. Location — "Where We Celebrate"
Venue name (Finca Comasema), ceremony and reception times, an embedded Google Maps placeholder, and an "Open in Maps" button.

### 5. Day Program (Timeline)
Vertical timeline with custom Lucide icons for each event:
- 16:00 — Ceremony
- 18:00 — Welcome Cocktail
- 20:30 — Dinner
- 22:30 — First Dance
- End of Party

### 6. Dress Code
Two side-by-side cards: "Military" (formal dress uniform) and "Civilians" (formal suit and gown), each with a subtle illustration or icon.

### 7. FAQ / Important Info
Clean accordions for: Bus Service, Pets, and Children — with placeholder answers.

### 8. RSVP Form
"Confirm Your Attendance" section with fields for Full Name, Attendance (Yes/No radio), Dietary Restrictions (text area), and a Sage Green "Send" button. Form data stored in local state (no backend needed for now).

---

## Technical Details
- **Mobile-first** responsive design
- **Scroll animations**: Sections fade in as the user scrolls into view
- **Smooth scrolling** between sections
- **Google Fonts**: Playfair Display + Great Vibes loaded via import
- **Lucide React** icons throughout
- **No backend required** — purely frontend
